Khaman

Khaman is a food common in the Gujarat state of India made from soaked and freshly ground channa dal or channa flour. Khaman is very similar to Dhokla.

List of Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan / gram flour
  • pinch of asafoetida
  • pinch of hing or asafoetida optional
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp ginger and chilli paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp Fruit Soda

Steps:

  1. take a bowl and add besan, hing, turmeric powder, ginger - chilli paste and salt.
  2. mix well with a help of whisk. now add yogurt, oil, little by little water and mix thoroughly.
  3. also add baking soda, baking powder and mix thoroughly.
  4. now grease the tray or tiffin box with oil.
  5. pour the mixture into the tray.
  6. place it in the steamer and steam for 10-15 mins

Dabeli

Dabeli, kutchi dabeli or double roti originated in Kachchh region of Gujarat.
